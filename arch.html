Architecture & Time

An architecture that rises and sinks back into fluidity, into the turbulence of a continually changing matrix of conditions, into an eternal, ceaseless flux. We all experience, sense, embrace or repudiate the phenomena of space and time.

As spatial inhabitants and transient visitors, we experience a small moment in time that is only capable of expressing an often limited perspective of an entire history of a place.

25 Artists join together to create according to the challenging prompt regarding spatial expression. All mediums and experiences welcomed via a public call and design brief organized and created by @c_mplx
